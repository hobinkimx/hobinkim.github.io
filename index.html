About Me

I'm a PGY-1 resident physician specializing in Psychiatry at RUHS (Riverside University Health System), California. I received my Doctor of Medicine (MD) degree from Loyola University Chicago Stritch School of Medicine and earned my BA in Mathematics from Pomona College. I also conducted research at Stanford University, contributing to several peer-reviewed publications.

I'm happy to help with applications to college and medical school — especially for first-gen, immigrant, or underserved students.
